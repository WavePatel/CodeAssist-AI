About CodeAssist AI

CodeAssist AI is a startup project focused on making home design and building code guidance more accessible. We built this platform to help homeowners, architects, and structural engineers get practical, location-aware recommendations quickly.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify construction decision-making by combining AI support with clear, professional guidance. We aim to reduce confusion around permits, renovation planning, and code-related questions.
